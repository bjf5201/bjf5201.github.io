A little bit about me.

I am in my junior year at UNC Wilmington where I am studying Information Technology.

Born and raised in central North Carolina, I have loved tinkering with computers for as long as I can remember. I began making websites as a teenager and I fell in love with coding. I created my first website at the age of eleven, which was a fansite dedicated to Hilary Duff called "Hilary Heaven.com". I taught myself front end development languages through a process of trial, error, and a lot of Googling. Stack Overflow and Reddit became my best friends. After about a year I had taught myself HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

I took some time off from web development to pursue another passion of mine, acting, for seven years. While I was away from the world of the web, I still found myself creating graphics and websites to promote various shows and projects that I was a part of. I founded an acting company, Studio A, with a few of my friends, and I oversaw the creation of our website and our social media.

In 2018 I returned to school at Cape Fear Community College where I graduated with Honors. I then transferred to UNC Wilmington in the Fall of 2020, where I made the Deans List my first semester.

It was about a year ago that I dusted off my keyboard and threw myself back into web development. What was once something I played around with for fun has now become somewhat of an obsession. I've polished my HTML, CSS, and JavaScript skills and have learned Python and C# while studying at UNCW. The Bash command line is my preferred method of interacting with my machine and I'm learning how to write my own scripts for the shell.

I am eager to learn even more. What excites me about IT is that you simply never stop learning. The knowledge base grows exponentially with each day. I believe I would bring the proper technical skills and determination required for any entry-level IT role in an organization.
